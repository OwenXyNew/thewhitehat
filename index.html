The White Hat

I Need the Hat

So, everyone knows about your famous white hat. I said I would write a 5-page essay, but I decided against it and made a website instead. I'm using HTML code from what I learned from freeCodeCamp which I have been enjoying. I like making things harder on myself for no reason, so let's get started!

  1. It protects your eyes:

    The sun can make it hard to see, and it can harm your eyes. Also, the hat will protect you from sunburns! (Not that I have to worry about that.)

  2. It's stylish:

    Your hat has style. There's no question about it. It looks awesome.

  3. I'm broke:

    I would buy my own, but you know I don't have that money. You have a job you mostly enjoy, you get to teach a whole bunch of crazy kids while I get to have them as friends. How I earn money is by doing extra chores. I don't have time for that, I have schoolwork, so I have to do that instead of earning money.

  4. Everyone wants a student to have it:

    Every MMS student wants your hat. It is iconic, even if they don't have you as a teacher, they like you.
